# Adir Morgan

I'm a PhD student at the Technion, Israel.
My interests are all kinds algorithms and their intersection with the physical world.

My current research is focused on the application of graph algorithms and combinatorial optimization to robotics motion planning.
You can read more about my research interests [here]().

Prior to my PhD I was an algorithms R&D engineer at Samsung, [Trieye](https://trieye.tech/) and [Solidus](https://www.solidus-tech.com/).
